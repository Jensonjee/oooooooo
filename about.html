hola.

I'm Jenson Jose from Chalakudy, a vibrant town I proudly call home, nurtured by a small yet incredibly supportive family. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Christ College, Calicut University, where I embarked on a transformative journey of learning and innovation. A project dear to my heart was creating a system that converts audio into sign language, aimed at empowering the deaf community. This experience deepened my belief in the power of technology to create meaningful change. Beyond coding, I explore my creative side through video and photo editing, bringing ideas to life visually. I’m also a movie enthusiast and love discovering new places, drawing inspiration from every experience. I’m passionate about using my skills to contribute to projects that make a difference and bring creativity to life in exciting ways.
